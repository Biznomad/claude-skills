## Success criteria per phase

**Phase 1 — Foundation**
- Brand bible complete with: positioning, ideal member profile, promise, offer ladder, voice
- Founder has approved the brand bible

**Phase 2 — Social presence**
- Bio copy written for all 4 platforms (IG, TikTok, YT, X)
- Profile photo and cover image brief written
- Linktree structure defined
- All story highlights named and briefed

**Phase 3 — Content engine**
- 30-day calendar with themes and pillar assignments for each post
- At least 12 content pieces fully written: hooks, scripts, captions, CTAs
- Content covers all 3 pillars: Attract (50%), Nurture (30%), Convert (20%)
- At least 2 pieces per active channel

**Phase 4 — Skool setup**
- About page copy written
- 5 feed categories named and described
- Classroom structure with all module names and lesson outlines
- Gamification levels configured
- Welcome DM written
- Founder intro post written

**Phase 5 — Funnels**
- Lead magnet concept defined and outlined
- All link-in-bio structure defined
- At least 5 social→Skool convert content formats written
- Email/DM nurture sequence (7 messages) written if using email

**Phase 6 — Launch**
- Launch checklist complete (all ✅)
- First 7 community posts drafted
- Founder has reviewed and approved all artifacts in outbox/
- Skool community is live or ready to go live
