## 3. Social → Skool conversion content (Convert pillar)

These are the specific content formats that move people from "follower" to "member."

### The "Community teaser" post
"I can't put this in a public post — so I put it inside {{community_name}} for our members.
If you want [specific thing], join us here: [link]"
Frequency: 1× per week

### The "Live call" clip
Record your weekly Skool call. Post a 60-second clip of the most valuable moment.
Caption: "We go deeper on this every [day] inside {{community_name}}. Link in bio."
Frequency: after every live call

### The "Member win" post
"This member joined {{community_name}} [X] days ago. Here's what happened..."
[Specific, concrete result with permission]
"[Community link in bio]"
Frequency: 1× per 2 weeks (or whenever a win comes in)

### The "Behind the curtain" story
Show the community feed, a classroom module, or a live call screenshot (faces blurred).
Caption: "Here's what's happening inside {{community_name}} right now..."
Frequency: weekly on Stories

### The direct invite (DM outreach)
When someone engages with your content (likes, comments, shares):
"Hey [name] — saw you [engaged with X post]. That resonated? I think you'd love
{{community_name}} — it's where I go deeper on [topic]. Want me to send you the link?"

## 4. Email / DM nurture sequence (if using lead magnet)

Send over 5-7 days after the free download:

| Day | Subject / First line | Content |
|-----|---------------------|---------|
| 0 (instant) | "Here's your [lead magnet name] 🎁" | Deliver the thing. Thank them. |
| 1 | "The one thing most people miss about [topic]" | Short teaching. No pitch. |
| 3 | "Can I share something personal?" | Founder story. Vulnerable. Real. |
| 5 | "[Name], have you tried [specific action]?" | Engagement email. Ask a question. |
| 7 | "Here's where I take this further..." | Invite to {{community_name}}. Soft pitch. |
| 10 | "One last thing..." | Hard pitch. Clear CTA. Deadline or reason to act now. |

**Email platform options:** ConvertKit, Beehiiv, or Mailchimp (free tier)
Set up automation → "When someone downloads [lead magnet] → start sequence"

## 5. Challenge funnel (most effective for community launches)

A 5-7 day free challenge that ends with a paid community invite.

**Structure:**
- Day 1: Welcome + the big promise (what we'll accomplish together)
- Days 2-5: One action per day (each builds on the last)
- Day 6: Results + celebration (collect wins from participants)
- Day 7: The invitation — "You've done the free challenge. {{community_name}} is
  where we keep going. Join us: [link]" — time-sensitive offer (48hr window)

**Where to run the challenge:**
- Instagram Stories + Lives (broadest reach)
- Email + Telegram group (highest engagement)
- Or directly inside a free Skool group → paid upgrade at the end
