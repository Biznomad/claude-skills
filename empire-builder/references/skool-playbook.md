# Skool Playbook — How to Build and Grow a Skool Community

Reference for the empire-builder skill. Read when generating Skool setup content (Phase 4)
and when advising on community structure, gamification, and growth.

---

## What Skool actually is

Skool is a community + courses platform. A Skool "group" has:
- **Community feed** — like a Facebook group, but cleaner. Members post, comment, react.
- **Classroom** — where you put courses, modules, PDFs, videos. Free members can see
  the feed; paid course access is gated by membership tier or points.
- **Members page** — leaderboard and profiles.
- **Events** — live calls, workshops, group coaching (Zoom/Google Meet embedded).
- **Gamification** — points for posting, commenting, completing lessons. Points unlock
  access to course levels (configure: 0 pts → Level 1, 50 pts → Level 2, etc.)

Skool takes 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction + $99/mo platform fee (as of 2026).

## Paid vs free — how to decide

| Factor | Free community | Paid community |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| Growth speed | Faster (no friction to join) | Slower (price barrier) |
| Member quality | Mixed | Higher intent |
| Revenue | From back-end offers only | Immediate MRR |
| Content commitment | Lower bar | Must deliver value |
| Best for | Audience-building first, monetize later | Founders with proven value |

**Recommendation for manifestation/entrepreneur niche:** Start paid ($27-$97/mo) if the
founder already has ANY proof (testimonials, her own transformation story). The audience
self-selects for people who invest in themselves — exactly who you want.

## Community structure (what to set up on Skool)

### Categories to create in the feed
1. **📢 Announcements** — only admin can post; for updates and new content drops
2. **👋 Introductions** — every new member posts here (required); triggers welcome DMs
3. **✨ Wins & Gratitude** — daily/weekly celebrations; social proof goldmine
4. **❓ Questions & Support** — members help each other; founder answers 2x/week
5. **📚 Resources** (optional) — member-shared tools, books, links

### Classroom module structure (for manifestation + entrepreneur niche)
Build 5-7 modules. Each module = 3-7 short lessons (10-15 min max). Framework:
- Module 1: Foundation / Why [topic] works (belief + quick win)
- Module 2: The system / framework (your method)
- Module 3: Practical application (do the thing)
- Module 4: Obstacles + troubleshooting (the real work)
- Module 5: Level up / advanced (for engaged members)
- Bonus module: Live call replays / community-created content

### Gamification setup
Points per action (configure in Skool settings):
- New post: 2 pts
- Comment: 1 pt
- Reaction received: 0.5 pt
- Lesson completed: 5 pts
Level unlocks:
- Level 1 (0 pts): Access to feed + Module 1
- Level 2 (25 pts): Module 2-3
- Level 3 (75 pts): Module 4-5
- Level 4 (150 pts): Bonus content + early access

## Growth mechanics (what actually moves the member counter)

### Week 1-4 (0 → first 10 members)
- Founder posts in the community **every single day** — even when it feels like talking
  to no one. This builds the habit AND creates searchable content when new members arrive.
- Direct outreach: DM 5-10 people personally. Not a pitch — "I started something and
  thought of you. Want a free look?"
- Leverage existing network first: friends, family, existing social followers.

### Month 2+ (10 → 50 members)
- Content on social → points to community → first conversion.
- The content hook: "I can't share this in a public post, so I put it in [Community Name].
  Join here: [link]"
- Referral mechanic: existing members get 1 month free for each referral.
- Weekly live call (even 30 min) keeps retention high and generates clips for social.

### Month 3+ (50 → 100+ members)
- Member spotlight posts on social (with permission) — best testimonials.
- Free challenge (5-7 days) that ends with a pitch to join the paid community.
- Affiliate program: members who refer 3+ people get elevated status/access.

## Skool SEO (how people find communities organically)

- Community name and description should contain the **exact words** your ideal member
  would type into Skool's search bar.
- Use keywords naturally: "manifestation," "law of attraction," "entrepreneur,"
  "mindset," "build a business," "abundance."
- Community cover image should include your name/community name as text (not just art).
- A filled-out, active community ranks higher in Skool's discovery algorithm.
