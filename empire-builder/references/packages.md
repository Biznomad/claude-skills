# Empire Builder — Resale Packages

How to package and price the empire-builder skill as a productized service.
The skill itself is free (it's yours). What you sell is YOUR TIME + EXPERTISE
deploying it for another founder's community buildout.

---

## The service model

You are an AI-powered community strategist. The empire-builder skill is your backend.
You sell done-for-you community launches and growth loops — you charge for setup,
strategy, and ongoing management. The skill runs in YOUR Claude Code; the client
never sees the code.

## Deliverables by tier

### Starter — Community Launch ($997 one-time)
- ✅ Founder interview (1 session via Loom or Zoom)
- ✅ Brand bible (`outbox/brand-bible.md`) — positioning, niche, promise, offer ladder
- ✅ Social profile copy for all 4 platforms (IG, TikTok, YT, X)
- ✅ 30-day content calendar with 12 ready-to-post pieces
- ✅ Skool setup guide (community structure, categories, gamification config, welcome DM)
- ✅ Lead funnel (lead magnet concept + link-in-bio setup)
- ✅ 1 revision round on all deliverables
- ✅ Handoff call: walk through outbox, how to publish everything
- ❌ No ongoing growth loop
- ❌ No Telegram reporting

### Builder — Launch + Growth ($997 setup + $497/mo for 3 months)
Everything in Starter, plus:
- ✅ 2 growth loop increments per month (1 acquisition, 1 community/showcase)
- ✅ Monthly content batch (8 pieces per month)
- ✅ Monthly Telegram progress card with metrics
- ✅ Skool optimization suggestions based on engagement data
- ✅ 3-month minimum; renews monthly after

### Operator — Full Empire ($2,997 setup + $997/mo)
Everything in Builder, plus:
- ✅ Weekly growth loop runs (4 per month)
- ✅ Full content production (12-16 pieces/month)
- ✅ Skool classroom module development (1 new module per month)
- ✅ Referral program design + member spotlight system
- ✅ Monthly metrics dashboard (members, churn, MRR, content engagement)
- ✅ 60-min monthly strategy call
- ✅ Telegram real-time changelog (every increment delivered to founder's DM)

### Agency — White Label ($4,997 setup + $1,997/mo)
Everything in Operator, plus:
- ✅ Builds out 1 client's community under your brand/agency
- ✅ You onboard via your own interview process (we train you)
- ✅ White-labeled deliverables (your logo, your brand)
- ✅ Operator training: 2 sessions teaching you the skill and how to run it
- ✅ Unlimited revisions during setup month

## Onboarding checklist (for you as the operator)

When a new client signs up, run through this with them:

1. [ ] Send payment link, collect deposit (50% upfront standard)
2. [ ] Send the onboarding form (collect: name, community name idea, niche description,
       existing handles, Telegram username for reports)
3. [ ] Run `/empire-builder <slug>` — start the interview
4. [ ] Schedule a 30-min video call to do the interview live (better than async)
5. [ ] After interview, show them `example.json` filled in with their answers — confirm
6. [ ] Deliver Phase 1-2 artifacts within 5-7 business days (Starter)
7. [ ] Handoff call: walk through every outbox file, how to publish
8. [ ] For Builder+: schedule first growth loop run for Week 3 post-launch
9. [ ] Set up Telegram bot for changelog reporting (if Operator tier)

## Client handoff deliverables (what goes in the final package)

Zip everything in `clients/<slug>/outbox/` plus:
- `HANDOFF.md` — a plain-language guide to every file and what to do with it
- `PUBLISH-CHECKLIST.md` — step-by-step for posting each piece of content
- `SKOOL-SETUP.md` — exact steps to configure the Skool community (screenshots if helpful)
