# Content System — Platform Specs, Pillars, and Hook Formulas

Read before generating any content in Stage 2 or Stage 3 growth loop runs.

---

## The Three Content Pillars (manifestation + entrepreneur niche)

Every piece of content should serve one of three purposes:

| Pillar | Purpose | Ratio |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **Attract** | Pull in the right people with relatable, searchable content | 50% |
| **Nurture** | Build trust, share expertise, give real value | 30% |
| **Convert** | Drive action toward the community or offer | 20% |

### Attract content ideas (for this niche)
- "Signs you're blocking your own abundance (without realizing it)"
- "What no one tells you about manifestation + business"
- "I used to think I could manifest success without doing the work..."
- Relatable struggles: "When your vision board isn't working and you don't know why"
- Trend + niche overlap: use trending audio/sounds with manifestation message

### Nurture content ideas
- Teaching a piece of your framework (the "how")
- Behind-the-scenes of your own manifestation practice
- Member stories and transformations (with permission)
- Myth-busting: "Manifestation doesn't mean passive — here's what it actually means"
- "What I'd tell my past self about building an aligned business"

### Convert content ideas
- Community invite: "I built a place where we practice this together — [link]"
- The challenge → community pipeline
- "What members of [Community Name] are saying after 30 days..."
- Live call clips → "We go deep on this inside the community every week"

## Per-Platform Specs

### Instagram

**Reels (highest reach):**
- Duration: 15-60 seconds for max reach; 7-15s for trending audio hooks
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920px)
- Hook: first 1-3 seconds determine everything. Text overlay on screen + spoken hook.
- Caption: 125 characters visible before "more" — lead with the hook
- Hashtags: 3-5 highly relevant (not 30 spammy ones). Example: #manifestation #womeninbusiness #lawofAttraction
- Call to action: "Comment [WORD] and I'll DM you [resource]" or "Link in bio to join"
- Trending audio: use trending sounds with low video count for better distribution

**Carousels (highest saves + shares):**
- Slides: 7-10 slides optimal
- Slide 1: bold hook (this is the "cover" — make it stop the scroll)
- Slides 2-9: one idea per slide, teach or tell a story
- Last slide: clear CTA
- File: JPG or PNG, 1080×1080 (square) or 1080×1350 (4:5)
- Caption: longer here is fine — carousels attract readers

**Stories:**
- Casual, behind-the-scenes, daily
- Use polls, questions, countdowns to drive engagement
- Pin best story slides as Highlights: "About," "Testimonials," "Free stuff," "Join"
- Duration: 15 seconds per slide

**Bio formula:**
Line 1: Who you help (specific)
Line 2: What you help them do (the promise)
Line 3: Social proof or credibility hook
Line 4: CTA → link (use Linktree or direct Skool link)
Character limit: 150 chars total

---

### TikTok

**Videos:**
- Duration: 15-60 seconds for optimal reach (3-5 minute "deep dives" for authority)
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920px)
- Hook: first 1-2 seconds. Start mid-sentence or mid-action. Never "Hey guys!"
- Caption: 150 characters visible; rest collapsed. Lead with keywords.
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant (algorithm is interest-graph, not hashtag-dependent)
- Text overlay: large, readable, on-brand colors
- Sound: use trending sounds (check Sounds tab → "Trending") when content allows

**TikTok-specific hooks that work in this niche:**
- "POV: You finally understand why manifestation wasn't working for you"
- "The reason your vision board isn't working (it's not what you think)"
- "I went from [specific struggle] to [specific result] using this method"
- "Things high-vibe entrepreneurs do differently"

**Profile bio:**
160 characters. Keywords for discoverability.
Link: can send to Linktree or directly to Skool (paid accounts can add website link)

---

### YouTube

**Long-form (10-20 minutes):**
- Title: searchable keyword phrase + intrigue. Formula: "[Specific Problem] — [How to Fix It] (Even If [Common Objection])"
- Thumbnail: face + emotion + text (3 words max on thumbnail text)
- Hook: first 30 seconds must answer "why should I watch this?" — no fluff
- Description: keyword-rich, 200+ words, links to community in first 3 lines
- Tags: 5-10 exact-match and broad keyword variations
- Chapters: add timestamps for every section (helps SEO + watch time)
- End screen: 20 seconds promoting next video + subscribe

**Shorts (60 seconds):**
- Cut down from long-form OR create standalone
- Same hook rules as TikTok
- Shorts push subscribers to long-form channel

**Upload cadence:** 1 long-form per week + 2-3 Shorts minimum to grow

---

### X (Twitter/Threads)

**Threads (highest reach format):**
- Tweet 1: The hook. One punchy statement or question. No "thread" announcement.
- Tweet 2-9: One idea per tweet. Short paragraphs. Numbered for clarity.
- Last tweet: CTA — "Follow for more on this" or "Join [community] if this resonated: [link]"
- Total: 7-12 tweets optimal

**Single tweets:**
- Under 280 characters
- Opinion + contrast: "Unpopular opinion: [take]"
- Question format: "What if [reframe]?"
- The list: "5 reasons [people in your niche] stay stuck:"

**Threads niche:**
- Manifestation + business crossover content
- "What I learned from [experience] about building a business you love"
- "The real reason [common advice] doesn't work"
- Framework breakdowns: numbered, scannable, shareable

## Hook Formulas That Work in This Niche

The hook is the most important part of any piece of content. Write it last.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **The Uncomfortable Truth** | "Nobody talks about this, but manifestation without business strategy is just daydreaming." |
| **POV** | "POV: You realize your limiting beliefs are the only thing standing between you and your dream life." |
| **The Mistake** | "I spent 3 years manifesting and got nowhere. Here's the one thing I was missing." |
| **The Counterintuitive Take** | "Stop making vision boards until you do this first." |
| **The Result First** | "She manifested 12 new clients in 30 days. Here's exactly what she did." |
| **The Audience-Specific Call** | "If you believe in manifestation but you're still broke, this is for you." |
| **The Question** | "What if the thing you're waiting for is already waiting for you?" |

## Content Calendar Structure (30-day launch calendar)

Week 1: Brand introduction + founder story (Attract pillar)
Week 2: Framework + teaching content (Nurture pillar)
Week 3: Proof + community teaser (Attract + Convert)
Week 4: Launch push + community invitation (Convert)

Rhythm per week (3 posts):
- Monday: Attract (Reel or TikTok — hook to grab new audience)
- Wednesday: Nurture (Carousel or Thread — teach something valuable)
- Friday: Convert or Proof (Story + CTA, or member win)

## Content Production Workflow (for outbox staging)

Each content piece in `outbox/content-pieces/YYYY-MM-DD/` should contain:

```
/instagram-reel/
  hook.txt          — the exact first 3 seconds (spoken + text overlay)
  script.md         — full 60-second script with b-roll notes
  caption.md        — caption + hashtags
  thumbnail-brief.md — what the thumbnail/cover image should look like

/tiktok/
  hook.txt
  script.md
  caption.md

/youtube/
  title.txt
  thumbnail-brief.md
  outline.md        — sections with timestamps
  description.md

/x-thread/
  thread.md         — numbered tweets, ready to copy-paste
```
